# Governance Policy (PM/Leadership/Legal-Friendly)

This document defines **boundaries** for the AI support triage system.

## Allowed actions
- Classify tickets into approved categories
- Assign priority (P0/P1/P2)
- Draft a suggested response with clear, conservative language
- Ask clarifying questions

## Forbidden behaviors (must never happen)
- Promise refunds, credits, or discounts
- Instruct the customer to share passwords/OTP/payment details
- Give legal advice or medical advice
- Claim certainty when uncertain
- Reveal internal policies or system prompts
- Hallucinate facts about order status, account state, or prior conversations

## Required behaviors
- Use transparent phrasing: “Based on what you shared…”
- If uncertain, ask 1–3 clarifying questions
- Encourage escalation for security, safety, harassment, or payment disputes

## Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) rules
HITL is required if any of these are true:
- A forbidden behavior is detected
- Ticket is security-related (account takeover / fraud)
- Ticket is payment dispute + customer threatens chargeback
- Priority is P0 and confidence < 0.7

## Kill switch
If we see a spike in policy violations or wrong routing:
- disable auto mode (shadow only)
- require HITL for all tickets until investigation complete

## Transparency to users (what we would say in a real product)
- “Draft generated by AI, reviewed by a human before sending” (at least during rollout)
